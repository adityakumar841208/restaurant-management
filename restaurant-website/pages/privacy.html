1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, Contact details (e.g., phone number, email address), Delivery address (if applicable), Payment information (e.g., UPI transaction ID).

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Order Policies

Order Fulfillment: Once an order is placed, it will be processed and ready for pickup or delivery within 15 minutes.
